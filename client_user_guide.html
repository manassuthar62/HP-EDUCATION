HP Education Portal - User Guide

A complete manual for managing your education institute efficiently.

1. Accessing the Portal

You can access the portal from any device (Desktop, Laptop, or Mobile) via the following URL:

Portal Link: https://hp-education.onrender.com
  • Login: Enter your Username and Password provided by the administrator.
  • Security: Always keep your credentials private.

2. Getting Started (First Time Setup)

Before adding students, follow this Required Order:

  1. Create Courses: Define the subjects/courses offered.
  2. Create Batches: Set up timings for those courses.
  3. Add Students: Enroll students into those pre-created courses and batches.

3. Academic Management

Courses Management

  • Go to Courses -> Add Course.
  • Set Course Name, Duration, and Fees.

Batch Management

  • Go to Manage Batches.
  • Create batches (e.g., "Morning Batch - 9 AM") and assign them to courses.

4. Student Management

  • New Admission: Go to Students -> Add Student.
  • Mandatory: Select the correct Course and Batch from the dropdowns.
  • Details: Fill in student information and set the total fee agreed upon.

5. Fee & Installment Management

The system automatically tracks payments and calculates remaining installments for you.

Recording a Payment (Installment)

  • Go to Fees -> Record New Payment.
  • Auto-Calculation: When you select a student, the system will automatically show the Next EMI/Installment amount.
  • Payment Method: Select Cash, UPI, or Bank Transfer.
  • Save & Receipt: Click Save to update the balance and generate a digital record.

6. Fee Reminders

  • Go to the Reminders section in the sidebar.
  • Upcoming Dues: See a list of students whose next installment is due soon.
  • Follow-up: View student Contact Numbers directly for quick calling or messaging.
  • Quick Action: Click Collect Fees to instantly record a payment for that student.

7. Reports & Analytics

  • Use the Reports section to see overall performance.
  • Download student lists and payment summaries as PDFs for your offline records.
